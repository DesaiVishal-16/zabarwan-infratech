Discover ZIPL’s foundation, leadership, and values — driving safe, high-quality infrastructure for national progress.

Leadership Messages :

Y.K. Jindal — Managing Director

“At Zabarwan Infratech Pvt. Ltd., we don’t just build structures; we build trust, relationships, and lasting legacies. Our client’s satisfaction is at the heart of every project, and our team is dedicated to surpassing expectations with unmatched workmanship, professionalism, and innovation. Together, we strive to make ZIPL a pioneer in the construction industry.”

Kuldip Singh Jamwal — Vice President (Ex-IAF Personnel)

“It’s a great privilege and honour to serve as Vice President at Zabarwan Infratech Pvt. Ltd., associated with the prestigious projects of the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). Our mission has always been to contribute towards nation-building by harnessing innovation, technology, and sustainable practices in infrastructure development.

The NHAI projects entrusted to us reflect not only the Government of India’s vision of creating world-class highways but also our team’s commitment to timely execution, uncompromised quality standards, and environmental responsibility. Roads are more than just a means of connectivity—they are the lifelines of economic progress, social integration, and national unity.

Through dedication, advanced technology, and professional expertise, Zabarwan Infratech Pvt. Ltd. strives to deliver excellence in every endeavor. As Vice President, I take pride in the relentless efforts of our team members, engineers, and partners who have made significant contributions to this journey of progress.

I am confident that with continued collaboration and support from all stakeholders, we will not only achieve but also surpass the expectations of NHAI. Together, we aim to create infrastructure that stands the test of time and paves the way for a brighter, more connected India.

Let us move ahead with renewed energy, innovation, and a shared vision of development.”

Our Story & Formation

Founded: 2023 (amalgamation of Yadvinder Kumar Jindal (HUF), AAY AAY Associates and other professionals).

Philosophy: Professionalism, discipline and a continual drive to achieve higher standards in civil engineering.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Deliver high-quality, safe, cost-effective infrastructure solutions that meet client expectations and contribute to national development.

Vision: To be recognized as a pioneer in the Indian construction industry — known for excellence, safety, and sustainable delivery.

Core Values

  • Safety: Prioritize life and health at every site.
  • Integrity: Transparent processes and fair dealings.
  • Teamwork: Cross-functional collaboration to deliver results.
  • Accountability: Measurable performance and responsibility.
  • Innovation: Adopt methods and machines that optimize outcomes.
  • Sustainability: Reduce environmental footprint and protect communities.
